About NutriBot

NutriBot is an AI-powered nutrition advisor built on IBM Watsonx.ai using the IBM Granite 3 8B Instruct model.

  • Personalized meal plans & calorie tracking
  • BMI & TDEE calculations (Mifflin-St Jeor)
  • Indian & international cuisine expertise
  • Family nutrition profiles & group planning
  • Meal nutritional analysis & healthiness ratings
  • Healthy recipe suggestions from your ingredients
⚠️ Not a substitute for professional medical or dietetic advice.
